Donate to covid emergency relief

As the COVID-19 crisis continues to loom over us, The Government of India has taken a strict combat measure by directing a lockdown of the entire nation. At this great hour of need, The Sparks Foundation, in close coordination with State Governments & District Administration, has stepped in to provide relief by providing bed and oxygen to thousands of people across the country.

People in India are courageously facing a much more infectious new wave/Tsunami of COVID that has hit even the remotest regions. Your support at this time of crisis can make a life or death difference. India needs you.
